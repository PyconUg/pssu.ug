About PSSU

Growing Python across East Africa

The Python Software Society of Uganda (PSSU) is the local chapter supporting the Python community in Uganda. We organise meetups, workshops, and outreach programmes to make Python accessible to everyone — from students to seasoned engineers.

In 2026, Uganda is proud to host PyCon Africa — the continent's largest gathering of Python enthusiasts. PSSU is leading the charge to ensure this is a welcoming, inclusive, and transformative event.
