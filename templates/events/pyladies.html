PyLadiesCon Africa @ PyCon Africa 2026

About

PyLadiesCon Africa is a dedicated program within PyCon Africa 2026 designed to empower and support women in the Python ecosystem across the continent.

The program creates a safe, inclusive, and inspiring space for women to learn, connect, and grow through technical sessions, mentorship, and community engagement.

Objective

To strengthen women's participation in Python by building confidence, skills, visibility, and leadership within the African tech ecosystem.

What to Expect

Inspiring Talks

From women in tech across Africa

Hands-on Workshops

Python workshops for all skill levels

Mentorship & Networking

Connect with mentors and peers

Speaking Opportunities

Opportunities to speak, learn, and contribute

Community Showcases

Engagement and community building activities

Who Should Apply

Women in tech (all experience levels)

Beginners interested in Python

Students and recent graduates

Career switchers

PyLadies chapter members

Underrepresented groups, including refugees

Impact

Increased participation of women in PyCon Africa

Stronger PyLadies chapters and networks

Growth of future speakers, mentors, and leaders
